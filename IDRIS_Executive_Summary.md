## ⚠️ Key Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Long Sales Cycles
**Issue:** Academic procurement takes 3-6 months  
**Mitigation:**
- Maintain diverse pipeline (20+ prospects always)
- Mix of quick wins (small projects) and strategic accounts
- Retainer model for predictable revenue
- Part-time income supplements initially if needed

### Risk 2: Client Concentration
**Issue:** Over-dependence on 1-2 major clients  
**Mitigation:**
- Target: No client >30% of revenue
- Two founders = two sales pipelines naturally
- Different domains = different client bases
- Active business development even when busy

### Risk 3: Founder Bandwidth
**Issue:** Only two people, can only do so much  
**Mitigation:**
- Clear capacity limits (70-80% billable target)
- Subcontractor network for overflow
- Say no to poor-fit projects
- Hire employee when revenue supports (Year 2)

### Risk 4: Market Competition
**Issue:** Universities building internal teams  
**Mitigation:**
- Partner approach (supplement, not replace)
- Specialized expertise they can't replicate
- Flexible surge capacity model
- Focus on institutions without internal teams

### Risk 5: Co-Founder Conflict
**Issue:** Disagreements on strategy or operations  
**Mitigation:**
- Clear roles and decision authorities
- Weekly alignment meetings
- Formal operating agreement with mediation clause
- Trial project first to test working relationship

### Vesting Schedule

**Standard 4-year vest, 1-year cliff:**
- Year 1: 25% vests
- Years 2-4: Monthly vesting (6.25% per year = 2.08% per quarter)

**Why:**
- Protects both founders if one leaves early
- Industry standard (credible to investors/acquirers later)
- Ensures long-term commitment

**Acceleration clauses:**
- Single-trigger on acquisition (common)
- Double-trigger if employment terminated post-acquisition (founder protection)

**Decision-Making:**
- Major decisions: Unanimous consent (raising capital, selling company, hiring, major expenditures >$25K)
